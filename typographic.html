THE CONCEPT

This project explores the intersection of brutalist typography and organic textures. By utilizing a constrained grid system, I focused on how letterforms can become architectural elements within a digital space.


The color palette was strictly limited to earthy tones—Terracotta and Bone—to ensure the focus remained on the hierarchy and weight of the "Outfit" and "Space Grotesk" typefaces.

VISUAL SYSTEMS

Every element follows a strict baseline grid. The generative AI elements were used to create the background grain, providing a tactile, "printed" feel to a high-resolution digital output.
